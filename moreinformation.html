Records

Attendance Radar is a mobile app that uses Bluetooth to track student attendance efficiently. Teachers send a signal, and students confirm their presence in the app. The attendance list can then be transferred onto the school's system manually or automatically.

uses

These systems can help reduce the time and effort required to record attendance, minimize errors, and provide more accurate and timely attendance data. They can also help improve security and prevent fraud by verifying the identity of the person present.

Features

* Provide multiple clocking methods, including biometric authentication, facial recognition, RFID cards, mobile apps, and web portals. Allow employees to clock in and out remotely. Track employee schedules and work hours accurately. Leave Management: * Facilitate leave request submission and approval workflows. Manage different types of leave, such as sick leave, vacation leave, and personal time off. Generate leave reports for payroll and compliance purposes.

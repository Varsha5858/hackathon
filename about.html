Advantages

An automated attendance system is a system that uses technology to track and record attendance data automatically. Instead of relying on manual methods such as taking roll calls or signing in a physical attendance sheet, an automated attendance system uses various technologies such as biometrics (such as fingerprint or facial recognition), RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) tags or barcodes, or GPS .
